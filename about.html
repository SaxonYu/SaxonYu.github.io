Why Cybersecurity?

Why cybersecurity, you might ask? Well, it's simple for me. I've always been into writing and messing with code, but to be honest, the whole Leetcode grind just wasn't my jam. I'm all about getting my hands dirty and seeing the immediate impact of what I do. That's exactly what cybersecurity gives me—it's the best of both worlds.

That unforgettable moment when I first managed to crack a root shell on a victim machine? It was a game-changer. (Obviously, all in a controlled environment, duh.) The mix of excitement and a deep sense of accomplishment marked a pivotal moment for me.
